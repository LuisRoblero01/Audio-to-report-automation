# Product Vision

## Problem Statement

Existing transcription tools often operate under subscription models or have restrictive free tiers. Users need a simple, pay-as-you-go solution to convert voice messages and audio files into structured, professional documents without technical friction.

## Solution

A Telegram-native bot that accepts audio input and returns processed documents in minutes. The interface is chat-based — no app to install, no complex setup.

## Target Use Cases

- **Lectures & Classes** — Structured study notes with key concepts, dates, and references
- **Business Meetings** — Professional meeting minutes with decisions, agreements, and action items
- **Conferences** — Concise summaries highlighting key takeaways
- **Voice Notes** — Clean transcriptions organized for readability
- **Briefings** — Action-oriented summaries with dates, links, and requirements

## Business Model

Credit-based prepaid system:
- Users purchase credit packages
- Each audio processing session consumes credits based on duration
- No monthly subscriptions — only pay for what you use
- Minimum credit consumption per session to prevent abuse

## Differentiators

- No strict duration limits (competitors often impose them)
- Multiple specialized output formats
- Custom format support via templates
- Chat-based interface (no learning curve)
- Pay-per-use (no recurring commitment)

## Target Audience

- Students processing lecture recordings
- Professionals documenting meetings
- Researchers organizing conference material
- Anyone who needs quick, reliable audio-to-document conversion
